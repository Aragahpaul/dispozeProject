Our Story

Dispoze is a registered company in Nigeria, established in 2010 to provide solutions to the waste management challenges facing Nigerians.
From a fleet of 5 modern waste disposal trucks and 20 staff in 2010, dispoze now has 40 modern waste disposal trucks and ejector trailers and 200 staff across Nigeria to collaborate with government at all
levels in providing prompt and efficient waste management services.
